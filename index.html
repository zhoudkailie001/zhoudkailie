Project Transform Lungi Garrison

The idea for this project was originated from previous unfinished projects (the likes of tap water, market, pavillion etc.), left by 15 Battalion past Commanding officers. In 2022, A new Commanding officer was posted to 15 RSALF Infantry Battalion Named "Lt Col Dr Zhoud-'Ph.D(FBC-USL)". Kailie who was recently confermed with Doctor in philosophy with peace and conflict and political science degree at Fourah Bay College --University of Sierra Leone that once called the anthem of West-Africa. He sold out Project Transform Lungi Garrison during the Battalion's end of years celebration with a powerful powerpoint presentation, which brought about a lot of attentions from invitees especially those on high table coupled with pledges from all corners of that event. It kick start on January 2023 and a lot of stakeholders keep to their words and fulfilled their promises. This has now become the face Kaffu Bullom and the Republic of Sierra leone armed force (RSLAF). You can view the full photos of PTLG by clicking Here.
